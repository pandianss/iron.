# IRON Product Constitution
> **Mandate**: Define the multi-domain product portfolio that bridges "Popular Adoption" to "Institutional Lock-in" without violating Kernel boundaries.

## 1. The Commercial Matrix
This matrix maps the 6 Governance Domains across the 4 Commercial Layers.
*   **Adoption (L1)**: "The Wedge" (Free/Cheap, High Velocity, UI-driven). Drives behavior.
*   **System (L2)**: "The Glue" (SaaS, Team-metric, Workflow-driven). Embeds operations.
*   **Platform (L3)**: "The Authority" (Enterprise, Compliance-driven). Formalizes power.
*   **Kernel (L4)**: "The Truth" (Infrastructure, Protocol-driven). The inescapable dependency.

| Domain | Layer 1: Adoption (Tool) | Layer 2: System (Ops) | Layer 3: Platform (Gov) | Layer 4: Kernel (Infra) |

## 2. Product Detail & Commercial Strategy

### Domain I: Execution & Discipline
*   **Adoption Wedge**: A "Habit Tracker" that feels like a game but actually writes cryptographic proofs of completion.
*   **Conversion Path**: User loves the tracker -> Manager sees the data -> Manager buys "Cadence Sync" for the team -> Org deploys logic via **Protocol Studio**.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   L1: Free (Data ingress).
    *   L2: Per-User SaaS.
    *   L3: Seat License (Developer/Admin).
    *   L4: Protocol Execution Gas (Consumption).

### Domain II: Performance & MIS
*   **Adoption Wedge**: Beautiful, private personal analytics. Captures the "Quantified Self" crowd.
*   **Conversion Path**: Personal dashboards aggregate into "Unit OKRs". The Platform (Console) becomes the only way to see the "Whole Truth".
*   **Monetization**:
    *   L3: **Console** is the high-value "Pane of Glass" for Executives.

### Domain III: Coordination & Structure
*   **Adoption Wedge**: "Role Cards" - A simple tool to define "What do I do?". Solves ambiguity.
*   **Kernel Dependency**: Uses `DelegationEngine` to ensure that every Role Card is actually a cryptographically signed delegation of authority.

### Domain IV: Compliance, Audit & Risk (Stratum IV)
*   **Institutional Role**: This is the **Enterprise Anchor**.
*   **Value Prop**: "Don't trust, Verify." The **Risk Heatmap** (L3) is backed by the **Audit Log** (L4).
*   **Lock-in**: Once a regulator accepts an Iron Audit Log, the institution cannot leave without regulatory friction.

### Domain V: Authority, Identity & Policy
*   **Adoption Wedge**: **Iron Wallet** (was Iron ID). A "Personal Authority System" that holds your Ed25519 keys and manages your digital succession.
*   **Kernel Dependency**: This is the root of `IdentityManager`. If we win the wallet, we win the user's sovereign root.

### Domain VI: Learning, Evolution & Intelligence
*   **Adoption Wedge**: Simple calculators ("If I save X, I get Y").
*   **Institutional Role**: The **Forecast Engine** (L3) runs the `SimulationEngine` (L4) on live data to predict "Institutional Health".
*   **Monetization**: Premium Intelligence/Prediction feeds.

## 3. Kernel Exposure Policy
*   **Never** expose raw JSON DSL (L4) to L1 users. L1 users see "Habits" and "Cards".
*   **Never** allow L2 Systems to bypass the Kernel. All "Team Syncs" must compile down to L4 Actions.
*   **Always** ensure L3 Platforms visualize L4 State directly (no separate DBs).

## 4. Success Criteria
1.  **Velocity**: 100k Active Iron IDs (L1/V).
2.  **Density**: Enterprise accounts activating >3 Domains.
3.  **Dependency**: % of Organization's "Truth" stored in L4 State.
